Finnish professional ice hockey centrer who plays for the Minnesota Wild in the National Hockey League. He previously played professionally in his homeland with Oulun Kärpät and HIFK of the Finnish SM-liiga. He was the ninth overall pick, selected by the Minnesota Wild, in the 2010 NHL Draft.
